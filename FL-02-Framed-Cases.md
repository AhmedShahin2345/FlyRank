### Case Study 1: AUC Welcome Party

**The Problem:** 
The university throws a massive welcome party every year, but it historically bleeds money. We needed a way to make it financially self-sustaining without hiking ticket prices for the student body.

**What I Did (and Decided):** 
Instead of sending out generic sponsorship decks begging for cash, I flipped the script. I pitched the party to Red Bull and Orange Egypt as a high-value "experiential activation lab." I sold them on exclusive, captive access to 4,500 Gen-Z students. I gave Red Bull complete control over the DJ booth infrastructure, and gave Orange the headline stage branding, turning them into the heroes of the night rather than just logos on a flyer.

**What Came Of It:** 
We secured 200,000 EGP in total value (cash and in-kind infrastructure), completely covering the event's overhead. The sponsors got exactly what they paid for: Orange walked away with 145,000 organic impressions on our co-branded Instagram Reels, and Red Bull got 3,200 direct engagement QR scans at their booth. The students just thought it was the best party of the year.

### Case Study 2: SU Ticket Sales Campaign

**The Problem:** 
University students notoriously wait until the absolute last minute to buy tickets for campus events. This causes massive logistical headaches, unpredictable headcounts, and cash flow issues for the Student Union.

**What I Did (and Decided):** 
I needed a way to incentivize early purchasing. I approached Talabat (a food delivery app heavily used by students) and negotiated a 120,000 EGP cash sponsorship. I used that money to aggressively subsidize early-bird tickets. The catch? Students had to use a specific Talabat promo code to unlock the discount. I drove traffic exclusively through a highly targeted email blast and a TikTok countdown blitz, creating artificial scarcity. 

**What Came Of It:** 
Early-bird tickets sold out in 48 hours, securing the Student Union's cash flow instantly and solving our logistical nightmare. On the sponsor side, Talabat saw 2,800 first-time app orders using our exclusive promo code within a two-week window. A complete, data-backed win for both sides.
